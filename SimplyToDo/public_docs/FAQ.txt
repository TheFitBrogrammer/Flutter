# Simply ToDo App FAQ

## Getting Started

### How do I download and install Simply ToDo?

Simply ToDo is available for download on the App Store. Just search for "Simply ToDo" and click "Get" to download.

### Is Simply ToDo free?

Yes, Simply ToDo is completely free to use and does not contain any in-app purchases or advertisements.

## Features

### What can I track with Simply ToDo?

With Simply ToDo, you can add, edit, or delete items or tasks, as well as add them to a calendar for reference. 

### How do I change which items show up under the "All Items" tab?

Go to the "Settings" menu in the upper left corner of the app, then select the "All Items Filter" drop-down.

### How do I change the default item/task "tag" when adding a new item/task?

Go to the "Settings" menu in the upper left corner of the app, then select the "ADefault Item Tag" drop-down.

## Troubleshooting

### What should I do if the app crashes?

If the app crashes, try restarting it or your device. If the problem persists, please uninstall and reinstall the app, or send a report to the developer at mulltechsoftware@gmail.com. 

### I found a bug, where should I report it?

If you've found a bug, please email us at mulltechsoftware@gmail.com with a description of the issue and any relevant screenshots.
